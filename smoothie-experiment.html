Smoothie Tolerance Experiment

Goal: find what makes smoothies hurt. Don't reproduce the painful version — start with the gentlest smoothie, then add back one variable at a time. Log each one. Space trials a few days apart.

How to log: Do the trial, then tap how your stomach felt — 😌 fine, 😬 mild, or 🤕 bad. The first trial that turns 😬/🤕 points at the culprit (see the readout at the bottom). Saves on your device automatically.

What a bad trial points to

  • Trial 0 already hurts → it's the fruit/fiber or the cold-ish blend itself; shrink portion further & try one fruit only, then talk to a dietitian.
  • Trial 1 (cold) hurts → temperature is a trigger. Keep smoothies room-temp / not iced.
  • Trial 2 (plant milk) hurts → likely the additives (gums, carrageenan, inulin/chicory). Use water or an additive-free milk.
  • Trial 3 (big & fast) hurts → it's volume + speed + air. Keep them small and sip slowly.
  • Trial 4 (more/other fruit or greens) hurts → it's FODMAP/fiber load. Keep low-FODMAP (strawberry), small amounts, no raw greens.
Not medical advice. If pain is severe or persistent, see a GI doc or dietitian.
