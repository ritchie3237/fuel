Tomorrow's Prep — Proof of Concept

~40 min total · Makes today's lunch + 2–3 breakfasts + 1 extra lunch + snacks · Dairy-free, vinegar-free, gluten-free

1 Overnight oats 5 min → fridge

Do this first so it sets while you cook. Make 2 jars. Per jar:
  • ½ cup GF rolled oats (the Bob's Red Mill you bought)
  • ½ cup coconut milk (carton) + a splash more if dry
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds · 1 tbsp almond butter · pinch of salt
  • A few sliced strawberries
Stir well, lid on, fridge. Ready in ~4 hrs / overnight. Keeps 3–4 days.
→ 2 training-day breakfasts

2 Roasted chickpeas 2 min hands-on, 25–30 min oven

Preheat oven to 400°F. Drain + rinse 1 can chickpeas, pat very dry with a towel, toss with olive oil + salt, spread on a tray. Roast 25–30 min until crispy. (Start these, then move on.)
→ Crunchy snack for a few days

3 Sweet potato + turkey hash ~20 min

Cast iron, medium-high, glug of olive oil:
  • Dice 2 sweet potatoes (~½-inch). In first, alone, lid on, ~8–10 min, stirring, until tender.
  • Push to edges, add 1 lb ground turkey to the center, break up, ~5–6 min until no pink.
  • Add ½ diced onion + 1 diced bell pepper, salt + pepper, 3–4 min more.
Cool, portion into 2–3 containers.
→ 2–3 breakfasts

4 Cook the chicken ~15 min

Season ~1 lb chicken with salt + pepper. Pan with olive oil, medium-high, ~6–7 min per side until 165°F (or bake at 400°F ~20 min). Rest 5 min, then slice.
→ Protein for 2 salads

5 Assemble today's lunch salad 5 min

In a bowl: arugula, sliced cucumber, sliced chicken, ½ can chickpeas, ½ avocado diced, a dollop of hummus, handful of almonds. Dress only when you eat it: olive oil + squeeze of lemon + salt.
  • Pack the second salad undressed in a container for tomorrow — keep dressing separate so the greens don't wilt.
→ Today's lunch + tomorrow's lunch

Don't forget

  • Dressing is lemon, never vinegar (vinegar = severe on your panel).
  • No feta / no dairy · no tomato.
  • Eggs stay a separate easy breakfast — keep them to ~3x/week.
  • Snacks ready: apple + almond butter, roasted chickpeas, strawberries + almonds.
