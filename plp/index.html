Pocono Lake Preserve · Off-Season Date Requests

Welcome!

This page is for scheduling submissions for the Pocono Lake Preserve house. Use the form below to request dates during the off-season. Your request will be sent to the scheduler automatically, and you'll be contacted to confirm.

  • Spring off-season: May and June
  • Fall off-season: September (starting the day after Labor Day) and October

You'll get a confirmation email when you submit. Once the submission window closes, all requests are reviewed together and the final schedule is published to the shared PLP Off-Season Schedule Google Calendar.

Date request(s)

Mark each one Primary (your preferred dates) or Backup (an alternative if your primary can't be accommodated), and say whether you'd want the house exclusively to yourselves for those dates — exclusive means no other family staying at the house. Add as many requests as you like.
